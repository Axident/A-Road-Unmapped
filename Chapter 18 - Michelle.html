“Hey Kevin,” I said into the phone in the truckers lounge, “its me Patrick.”

“Who?”

“Patrick,” I played along, “from high school. We lived 2 blocks apart for 6 years. We share cousins, and I stood up in your wedding 3 months ago?”

“Ha ha,” Kevin laughed, “how’s trucking?”

“Not bad, guess where I am?”

“Dude, are you here?!?”

“Yep, the truckstop just across the river.”

“Hold on, I’ll come get you!”

Kevin and his new wife lived in a cute little apartment near downtown Minneapolis. We chatted about what crazy changes were going on in our lives all the way back to his place. When I got there, his wife Laura was on the phone with a friend and seemed fairly concerned for her. Apparently Laura wanted to go cheer her up but Michelle, the girl on the phone, told her it was ok and they could talk later when they didn’t have company.

“Oh now I feel bad,” I said, “you should go comfort your friend.”

“I want to,” Laura looked so torn, “but I want to get to know Kevin’s childhood friend too!”

“Call her back,” I insisted, “you should at least finish your conversation, I can wait.”

Laura called her back and the conversation turned to explaining to Michelle who her company was. I can’t remember who had the idea first, but we were soon discussing driving the 3 of us to Michelle’s apartment.

“You’ll like her,” Laura was quite pleased with this plan, “she’s very sweet.”

“I hope she doesn’t mind me tagging along,” I felt a small bit like an extra wheel.

“Don’t worry about it,” this will be fun.

“Are you sure, you could just drop me off at my truck…”

“Don’t be silly, she’s cool.” Laura felt like I needed more convincing, “and she has huge boobs.”

“Well if she really needs people to come over and cheer her up, I don’t want to get in the way of that,” we all laughed.

The moment Michelle opened the door, I knew I’d like her. Aside from matching Laura’s description, her smile and laughter were infectious. She was embarrassed about her contacts prescription taking too long and she had to wear an old pair of glasses. I didn’t mind one bit, this girl was hot in glasses. The night went well and we actually exchanged phone numbers. I didn’t even know at the time, that this was the girl I’d seen at Kevin’s wedding.
